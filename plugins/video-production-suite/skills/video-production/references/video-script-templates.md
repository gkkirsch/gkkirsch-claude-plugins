# Video Script Templates

Complete, production-ready script templates for the five most common product video formats. Each template includes precise timing, shot types, narration, on-screen text, and music direction. Customize these for your product by replacing the bracketed placeholders.

---

## Template 1: Product Demo (60 Seconds)

**Style:** Developer tool / SaaS product demo (Linear, Cursor, Raycast style)
**Aspect Ratio:** 16:9
**Music:** Ambient electronic, confident, modern (100-115 BPM)
**Narration:** Voiceover (AI or recorded)

```
# VIDEO SCRIPT: [Product Name] — Product Demo

Platform: YouTube / Landing Page
Duration: 60 seconds
Aspect Ratio: 16:9
Style: Product Demo
Tone: Confident, modern, concise

---

## SCENE 1: The Hook (0:00 - 0:03)

SHOT TYPE: Screen recording — zoomed into the key UI interaction

VISUAL: [Show the single most impressive feature in action. The "wow moment"
plays immediately — no preamble. Example: A command palette opens, user types
3 words, and an entire page is generated instantly.]

NARRATION: "[Bold claim that summarizes the product's core value. Example:
'Ship features in minutes, not sprints.']"

ON-SCREEN TEXT: [Product Name] — [One-line tagline] (center, large, bold)

MUSIC/SFX: Music fades in — ambient electronic, steady pulse. Subtle UI click SFX.

TRANSITION: Cut to Scene 2

---

## SCENE 2: The Problem (0:03 - 0:12)

SHOT TYPE: Screen recording — realistic "before" scenario

VISUAL: [Show the painful old way of doing things. Example: A developer
switching between 4 different tabs, copy-pasting between tools, waiting for
a loading spinner, dealing with a cluttered UI. Make it feel relatable.]

NARRATION: "[Describe the pain point your audience feels. Example: 'You spend
hours jumping between tools, copying data, and waiting for builds. By the time
you ship, the feature is already outdated.']"

ON-SCREEN TEXT: [Pain point summary in 3-5 words] (lower-third, left-aligned)
Example: "Too many tools. Too slow."

MUSIC/SFX: Music stays low, slightly tense undertone. Keyboard typing SFX.

TRANSITION: Match cut — same screen position, but now showing the product

---

## SCENE 3: The Solution Introduction (0:12 - 0:20)

SHOT TYPE: Screen recording — product UI, clean and inviting

VISUAL: [Product opens for the first time. Clean dashboard or home screen.
Show the product's primary view with real-looking data. Cursor moves
purposefully to the first feature. Example: Clean project board with
organized tasks, no clutter.]

NARRATION: "[Introduce the product as the solution. Example: 'Meet [Product Name].
Everything you need to build, track, and ship — in one place.']"

ON-SCREEN TEXT: [Product Name] logo + tagline (center, fade in)

MUSIC/SFX: Music lifts — brighter tone, more energy. Subtle "reveal" chime.

TRANSITION: Smooth zoom into the first feature

---

## SCENE 4: Feature Showcase #1 (0:20 - 0:30)

SHOT TYPE: Screen recording — close-up on specific feature interaction

VISUAL: [Demonstrate the first key feature. Cursor moves deliberately.
Zoom into the interaction point. Show the input and the immediate result.
Example: User types a natural language command, AI generates a complete
project structure in real-time.]

NARRATION: "[Explain what is happening and why it matters. Example: 'Describe
what you want in plain English. [Product Name] builds it instantly — complete
with tests, docs, and deployment config.']"

ON-SCREEN TEXT: Feature name as a label (upper-left, small, persistent)
Example: "AI Project Generation"

MUSIC/SFX: Music continues building. Satisfying "completion" SFX when the
action resolves.

TRANSITION: Zoom out, slide to next feature

---

## SCENE 5: Feature Showcase #2 (0:30 - 0:40)

SHOT TYPE: Screen recording — medium shot showing feature in context

VISUAL: [Second key feature. Show a different aspect of the product.
Example: Real-time collaboration — two cursors on screen, changes syncing
instantly, inline comments appearing. Show the speed and fluidity.]

NARRATION: "[Example: 'Collaborate in real-time with your entire team.
Every change syncs instantly. No merge conflicts. No version confusion.']"

ON-SCREEN TEXT: Feature name label (upper-left)
Example: "Real-Time Collaboration"

MUSIC/SFX: Music steady, confident. Subtle notification sounds for
collaborative actions.

TRANSITION: Cut to wow moment

---

## SCENE 6: The Wow Moment (0:40 - 0:50)

SHOT TYPE: Screen recording — dramatic zoom into the most impressive capability

VISUAL: [THIS IS THE MOMENT. The single most impressive thing the product
does. Slow the pace slightly. Zoom in close. Let the viewer see every detail.
Example: One-click deployment — user clicks a button, a progress animation
plays for 2 seconds, then a live URL appears. The user clicks the URL and
the fully deployed application loads in a new tab.]

NARRATION: "[Let the visual breathe. Minimal narration. Example: 'One click.
Live in production.' Then silence for 2 seconds while the result displays.]"

ON-SCREEN TEXT: None — let the product speak. Or one word: "Done." (center, bold)

MUSIC/SFX: Music drops to minimal. Bass hit or low boom on the reveal.
2 seconds of near-silence to let the wow moment land.

TRANSITION: Dissolve to social proof

---

## SCENE 7: Social Proof + CTA (0:50 - 0:60)

SHOT TYPE: Text slide with brand colors, then logo

VISUAL: [Quick social proof — a stat, a logo wall of customers, or a
brief quote. Then transition to the logo, tagline, and CTA.
Example: "Trusted by 10,000+ teams" with a grid of recognizable company
logos. Then dissolve to: [Product Name] logo centered, URL below, and
"Start free today" button.]

NARRATION: "[Example: 'Join 10,000 teams already shipping faster with
[Product Name]. Start free at [website].']"

ON-SCREEN TEXT:
- "Trusted by 10,000+ teams" (center, medium)
- Then: [Logo] + [website URL] + "Start Free" (center, large)

MUSIC/SFX: Music swells to a confident finish. Clean ending — not a fade-out.

TRANSITION: End card holds for 3 seconds

---

END
```

## Template 2: Explainer Video (90 Seconds)

**Style:** Problem-solution format (Notion, Loom, Slack style)
**Aspect Ratio:** 16:9
**Music:** Friendly, warm, clear (95-110 BPM)
**Narration:** Warm, approachable voiceover

```
# VIDEO SCRIPT: [Product Name] — Explainer

Platform: YouTube / Website
Duration: 90 seconds
Aspect Ratio: 16:9
Style: Explainer
Tone: Friendly, clear, empathetic

---

## SCENE 1: The Relatable Problem (0:00 - 0:15)

SHOT TYPE: Mixed — lifestyle B-roll + screen recordings of the painful status quo

VISUAL: [Open with a relatable scenario. Example: A person at a desk surrounded
by sticky notes, 15 browser tabs open, Slack notifications piling up. Cut to
screen recording showing a messy spreadsheet, an overflowing inbox, a project
management tool with 200 overdue tasks.]

NARRATION: "You start every morning with good intentions. But by 10 AM, you
are buried in messages, drowning in spreadsheets, and wondering where the
day went. Sound familiar?"

ON-SCREEN TEXT: "Sound familiar?" (center, handwritten-style font, 0:13-0:15)

MUSIC/SFX: Light, slightly anxious undertone. Notification sound effects
layered for emphasis. Music is sparse and uncertain.

TRANSITION: Visual "reset" — screen wipes clean

---

## SCENE 2: There Has to Be a Better Way (0:15 - 0:25)

SHOT TYPE: Text slide transitioning to product introduction

VISUAL: [Clean transition — old chaos wipes away. Brief text slide:
"There's a better way." Then the product UI fades in, clean and organized.
The same data from the messy spreadsheet is now beautifully organized in
the product's interface.]

NARRATION: "What if all your work lived in one place? Organized automatically.
Updated in real-time. Always exactly where you need it."

ON-SCREEN TEXT: "There's a better way." (center, bold, clean font, 0:15-0:18)

MUSIC/SFX: Music shifts — brighter, more confident. A clean "chime" on
the product reveal.

TRANSITION: Smooth dissolve into the product demo

---

## SCENE 3: How It Works — Step 1 (0:25 - 0:40)

SHOT TYPE: Screen recording with numbered step indicator

VISUAL: [Show the first step of using the product. Screen recording with
a "Step 1" badge in the corner. Example: User creates a new workspace —
types a name, selects a template, and the workspace populates with
organized sections instantly.]

NARRATION: "Step one: Create your workspace. Pick a template or start fresh.
[Product Name] organizes everything automatically — projects, docs, tasks,
all connected."

ON-SCREEN TEXT: "Step 1: Create Your Workspace" (upper-left badge)

MUSIC/SFX: Steady, building energy. Subtle click SFX on interactions.

TRANSITION: Numbered wipe to Step 2

---

## SCENE 4: How It Works — Step 2 (0:40 - 0:55)

SHOT TYPE: Screen recording with numbered step indicator

VISUAL: [Second step. Example: User imports existing data — drag and drop
files, paste a URL, or connect an integration. Data flows in and auto-organizes.
Show the transformation from raw input to structured output.]

NARRATION: "Step two: Bring in your existing work. Import from anywhere —
spreadsheets, docs, even email. [Product Name] structures it all for you.
No manual organizing. No data entry."

ON-SCREEN TEXT: "Step 2: Import Everything" (upper-left badge)
Secondary text: "Spreadsheets, Docs, Email" (lower-right, small)

MUSIC/SFX: Music continues building. Satisfying "import complete" chime.

TRANSITION: Numbered wipe to Step 3

---

## SCENE 5: How It Works — Step 3 + Wow Moment (0:55 - 1:10)

SHOT TYPE: Screen recording — zoomed in for the key reveal

VISUAL: [Third step IS the wow moment. Example: User clicks "Generate Report."
AI analyzes all the imported data and produces a complete weekly report with
charts, summaries, and action items. Show the report appearing in real-time,
sections populating one after another.]

NARRATION: "Step three: Let [Product Name] do the heavy lifting. One click
generates reports, surfaces insights, and tells you exactly what needs your
attention today."

ON-SCREEN TEXT: "Step 3: Work Smarter" (upper-left badge)
Then: "Wow." or "Magic." (center, large, appears as the report generates)

MUSIC/SFX: Music peaks. Dramatic reveal SFX. Brief pause after the
moment lands.

TRANSITION: Zoom out to full product view

---

## SCENE 6: The Transformation (1:10 - 1:20)

SHOT TYPE: Split screen or before/after comparison

VISUAL: [Side-by-side comparison. Left side: the chaotic "before" from Scene 1
(messy desk, cluttered screen, notification overload). Right side: the organized
"after" with [Product Name] (clean dashboard, zero unread notifications,
clear task priorities). The contrast should be dramatic.]

NARRATION: "Remember that overwhelming morning? Here is your morning with
[Product Name]. Everything organized. Nothing missed. Ready to do your best work."

ON-SCREEN TEXT: "Before" (left side label) / "After" (right side label)

MUSIC/SFX: Music is warm and resolved. Feels like a deep breath.

TRANSITION: Dissolve to CTA

---

## SCENE 7: Social Proof + CTA (1:20 - 1:30)

SHOT TYPE: Text slide with brand elements

VISUAL: [Customer logos or testimonial quote. Then logo + CTA.
Example: "Join 50,000+ teams" with a row of recognizable logos. Dissolve to
centered [Product Name] logo, website URL, and CTA button.]

NARRATION: "Join 50,000 teams who have taken back their mornings. Try
[Product Name] free at [website]. No credit card required."

ON-SCREEN TEXT:
- "50,000+ teams" (with logo grid, 1:20-1:25)
- [Logo] + [URL] + "Try Free — No Credit Card" (1:25-1:30)

MUSIC/SFX: Music resolves cleanly. Final note rings out.

TRANSITION: End card holds for 3 seconds

---

END

## Template 3: Product Hunt Launch Video (30 Seconds)

**Style:** Maximum impact, visual-only (auto-plays muted on Product Hunt)
**Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 (1270x760 for Product Hunt gallery)
**Music:** Optional — most viewers will not hear it
**Narration:** NONE — all communication through visuals and text

```
# VIDEO SCRIPT: [Product Name] — Product Hunt Launch

Platform: Product Hunt
Duration: 30 seconds
Aspect Ratio: 16:9 (1270x760)
Style: Launch — Visual Only (no narration)
Tone: Bold, punchy, impressive

---

## SCENE 1: Logo + Tagline (0:00 - 0:03)

SHOT TYPE: Branded text slide

VISUAL: [Product logo centered on brand-colored background. Tagline fades in
below. Example: "[Product Name]" logo, then "The AI-powered workflow engine"
fades in beneath it.]

NARRATION: None

ON-SCREEN TEXT: [Product Name] + [Tagline] (center, large bold)

MUSIC/SFX: Optional — energetic opener if using audio

TRANSITION: Zoom into the product UI

---

## SCENE 2: Core Feature #1 (0:03 - 0:10)

SHOT TYPE: Screen recording in device mockup frame

VISUAL: [Primary feature demonstration. Use Screen Studio or Rotato for a
polished device frame. Show the most visually impressive interaction.
Zoom into the key UI element. Example: AI autocomplete filling in a complex
form in real-time. Everything should move — no static screenshots.]

NARRATION: None

ON-SCREEN TEXT: Feature name + one-line description (top or bottom,
large readable font, 32px+ equivalent)
Example: "AI Autocomplete — Fills forms in seconds, not minutes"

MUSIC/SFX: Driving beat. UI interaction sounds.

TRANSITION: Slide left to next feature

---

## SCENE 3: Core Feature #2 (0:10 - 0:17)

SHOT TYPE: Screen recording with annotation highlights

VISUAL: [Second feature. Different visual treatment than Scene 2 for variety.
Example: Drag-and-drop interface — user rearranges elements, connections
update automatically, a chart rebuilds in real-time. Add subtle highlight
circles or arrows pointing to key interactions.]

NARRATION: None

ON-SCREEN TEXT: Feature name + benefit (large, readable)
Example: "Visual Builder — Drag, drop, done"

MUSIC/SFX: Music continues. Satisfying snap/connect SFX.

TRANSITION: Cut to wow moment

---

## SCENE 4: The Wow Moment (0:17 - 0:24)

SHOT TYPE: Screen recording — hero shot

VISUAL: [The single most impressive thing. Slow down slightly. Give it
space. Example: User clicks "Launch" — a deployment progress bar fills,
analytics dashboard populates with live data, a success notification
appears. Everything happens smoothly in 5 seconds of screen time.]

NARRATION: None

ON-SCREEN TEXT: Minimal. Maybe one reaction word: "Instant." or no text
at all — let the product speak.

MUSIC/SFX: Music builds to peak. Impact SFX on the key moment.

TRANSITION: Dissolve to end card

---

## SCENE 5: CTA End Card (0:24 - 0:30)

SHOT TYPE: Branded end card

VISUAL: [Product logo centered. Below it: "Available now on Product Hunt"
with the Product Hunt logo/badge. Website URL at the bottom. Optional:
"Launching today — upvote and try free" with an upward arrow icon.]

NARRATION: None

ON-SCREEN TEXT:
- [Product Name] logo (center, large)
- "Available now on Product Hunt" (center, below logo)
- [website URL] (bottom center)
- Optional: Product Hunt badge/cat logo

MUSIC/SFX: Music resolves. Clean ending.

TRANSITION: Hold for 3 seconds, then loop or end

---

END
```

## Template 4: Tutorial / How-To Video (3 Minutes)

**Style:** Step-by-step educational walkthrough
**Aspect Ratio:** 16:9
**Music:** Calm, lo-fi, non-distracting (80-100 BPM)
**Narration:** Patient, clear, teacher-like voiceover

```
# VIDEO SCRIPT: How to [Task] with [Product Name]

Platform: YouTube
Duration: 3 minutes (180 seconds)
Aspect Ratio: 16:9
Style: Tutorial
Tone: Patient, clear, educational

---

## SCENE 1: Hook + Promise (0:00 - 0:10)

SHOT TYPE: End result preview — show the finished outcome

VISUAL: [Show the completed result of the tutorial FIRST. Example: A fully
deployed website, a completed dashboard, a working automation, a polished
design. Let the viewer see what they will build by the end.]

NARRATION: "By the end of this tutorial, you will have [specific outcome].
It takes about [time estimate], and you don't need any [prior experience/setup]."

ON-SCREEN TEXT: "What you'll build:" (top-left) with a screenshot or preview
of the final result

MUSIC/SFX: Calm lo-fi beat fades in. Warm and inviting.

TRANSITION: Cut to intro slate

---

## SCENE 2: Prerequisites (0:10 - 0:25)

SHOT TYPE: Text slide or screen recording of setup

VISUAL: [Brief checklist of what the viewer needs before starting. Show these
as animated checkboxes or a clean list. Example: "1. Free [Product Name]
account 2. A web browser 3. 5 minutes". If there is setup required, show the
signup flow quickly with 2x speed.]

NARRATION: "Before we start, here is what you need: [list prerequisites].
If you haven't signed up yet, there is a free tier — link is in the
description."

ON-SCREEN TEXT: Prerequisite checklist (animated items appearing one by one)

MUSIC/SFX: Music continues at low volume. Checkbox check SFX.

TRANSITION: Cut to Step 1

---

## SCENE 3: Step 1 (0:25 - 0:55)

SHOT TYPE: Screen recording with zoom and cursor highlights

VISUAL: [First step of the tutorial. Record at normal speed. Use zoom to
focus on the exact buttons, menus, and fields the viewer needs to interact
with. Show every click. Move deliberately. Example: Navigate to the
dashboard, click "New Project," fill in the project name, select settings,
click "Create."]

NARRATION: "[Walk through each action as you show it. Example: 'First,
head to your dashboard and click New Project in the top right. Give it
a name — I will call mine Demo Project. For the template, select Starter —
it gives us a clean foundation to build on. Click Create, and we are in.']"

ON-SCREEN TEXT: "Step 1: Create Your Project" (persistent badge, upper-left)
Key action callouts as needed: "Click here" with arrow (briefly)

MUSIC/SFX: Music stays low under narration. Click SFX on button presses.

TRANSITION: Numbered wipe or slide to Step 2

---

## SCENE 4: Step 2 (0:55 - 1:25)

SHOT TYPE: Screen recording — medium to close-up shots

VISUAL: [Second step. This is usually the "meat" of the tutorial — the main
task. Show the complete workflow. If it involves typing, show the typing in
real-time (or 1.5x speed for long inputs). Zoom into specific areas when
explaining sub-steps.]

NARRATION: "[Detailed walkthrough. Example: 'Now let us add some content.
Click the plus button to add a new section. You will see a menu with
different block types — we want the Text block. Start typing your content
here. Notice how the formatting toolbar appears when you select text.
Let us make this heading bold and increase the size.']"

ON-SCREEN TEXT: "Step 2: [Step Name]" (upper-left badge)
Tip callouts: "Pro tip: [shortcut or efficiency trick]" (lower-right, brief)

MUSIC/SFX: Music steady. Typing SFX if showing text input.

TRANSITION: To Step 3

---

## SCENE 5: Step 3 (1:25 - 1:55)

SHOT TYPE: Screen recording with split-screen or before/after

VISUAL: [Third step — often involves configuration, customization, or
connecting pieces together. Example: Customizing the appearance, adding
integrations, setting up automations. Show the cause and effect — change a
setting, immediately show the result.]

NARRATION: "[Example: 'For the final step, let us customize the look.
Head to Settings, then Appearance. I am going to switch to the dark theme —
watch how everything updates instantly. Now let us change the accent color
to match our brand. Looking good.']"

ON-SCREEN TEXT: "Step 3: [Step Name]" (upper-left badge)

MUSIC/SFX: Music builds slightly — approaching the finish.

TRANSITION: To result reveal

---

## SCENE 6: Result Reveal (1:55 - 2:20)

SHOT TYPE: Screen recording — pull back to show the full result

VISUAL: [Show the completed result. Zoom out to show the full picture.
If it is a website, show it in a browser frame. If it is a dashboard,
show all sections populated. If it is an automation, trigger it and show
it working. Take time to appreciate what was built.]

NARRATION: "And that is it. In just [X] minutes, we have built [describe
the result]. You can see [point out specific elements]. Everything is
[functional/live/working]."

ON-SCREEN TEXT: "Done!" or "Result:" (center, briefly)

MUSIC/SFX: Music lifts — satisfied, accomplished feeling. Success chime.

TRANSITION: Dissolve to bonus tips

---

## SCENE 7: Bonus Tips (2:20 - 2:45)

SHOT TYPE: Screen recording — quick montage of advanced features

VISUAL: [Show 2-3 advanced tips rapidly. Each gets 5-8 seconds. These are
"next level" tricks that reward viewers for watching the full tutorial.
Example: keyboard shortcuts, hidden features, workflow optimizations,
integration options.]

NARRATION: "A few bonus tips: [Tip 1 — show keyboard shortcut]. [Tip 2 —
show hidden feature]. And my favorite — [Tip 3 — show the power-user trick]."

ON-SCREEN TEXT: "Bonus:" label, then tip descriptions as they appear

MUSIC/SFX: Slightly faster pacing. Quick transition SFX between tips.

TRANSITION: Cut to CTA

---

## SCENE 8: CTA + Outro (2:45 - 3:00)

SHOT TYPE: Text slide / end card with subscribe elements

VISUAL: [End card with: subscribe button animation, video thumbnail for
"next tutorial", link to documentation/resources. Example: Left side shows
a "Watch Next" thumbnail, right side shows subscribe button and bell icon.]

NARRATION: "If this was helpful, hit subscribe for more tutorials like this
one. Check out [next video topic] — I will link it right here. And if you
get stuck, drop a comment below and I will help you out."

ON-SCREEN TEXT:
- "Subscribe for more tutorials" (left)
- Video thumbnail for next recommended video (right)
- [website URL] (bottom center)

MUSIC/SFX: Music resolves warmly. End.

TRANSITION: End card holds for 15 seconds (YouTube end screen duration)

---

END

**Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 or 1:1 (for social)

# VIDEO SCRIPT: [Customer Name] on [Product Name]

Duration: 45 seconds
Aspect Ratio: 16:9 (or 1:1 for social cut)

Tone: Authentic, warm, relatable

SHOT TYPE: Talking head (or text-over-B-roll if no camera footage)

NARRATION (Customer voice): "Before [Product Name], our team was spending

- Name, Title, Company (lower-third, appears at 0:02)

NARRATION (Customer voice): "We found [Product Name] and set it up in

adjective], and by the second day, we were already seeing results."

ON-SCREEN TEXT: "[Set up in X hours/days]" (center, bold, brief appearance)

MUSIC/SFX: Music lifts slightly — optimistic shift. Subtle transition SFX.

TRANSITION: Cut to results

---

## SCENE 3: The Transformation + Results (0:22 - 0:37)

SHOT TYPE: Split screen or data visualization

VISUAL: [Show measurable results. Before/after comparison, data
visualization, or the customer describing the impact. Example: Split screen
showing "Before: 3 hours per task" vs "After: 15 minutes per task" with
animated counters or a simple chart. Then cut back to the customer's face
for the emotional impact statement.]

NARRATION (Customer voice): "Now that same process takes [shorter time].

ON-SCREEN TEXT:

## SCENE 4: Recommendation + CTA (0:37 - 0:45)

SHOT TYPE: Talking head, then brand end card

VISUAL: [Customer delivers their genuine recommendation directly to camera.

NARRATION (Customer voice): "If you are dealing with [the problem], just

ON-SCREEN TEXT:

  — [Customer Name], [Title] at [Company] (0:37-0:42)
- Then: [Product Logo] + [Website URL] + "Try Free" (0:42-0:45)

MUSIC/SFX: Music resolves gently. Warm final note.

TRANSITION: End card holds for 3 seconds

## Template Usage Notes

### Adapting Templates to Your Product
1. Replace all bracketed `[placeholders]` with your actual content
2. Adjust timing based on the complexity of your product's UI
3. Add or remove scenes based on the number of features you need to showcase
4. Match the music mood to your brand personality
5. Test narration timing by reading aloud with a stopwatch

### Combining Templates
- Use Template 1 (Demo) as the core of a Template 4 (Tutorial) — embed a feature demo within a learning flow
- Create Template 3 (Product Hunt) by cutting down Template 1 (Demo) to its top 2 scenes
- Use Template 5 (Testimonial) as Scene 7 social proof within Template 2 (Explainer)

### Platform Reformatting
When adapting a 16:9 script to 9:16 (vertical):
- Increase all text sizes by 50%
- Reduce on-screen information density
- Add more close-up shots (screens are smaller)
- Shorten by 30-50% — vertical viewers have shorter attention spans
- Move all text overlays to the center of the frame (avoid top/bottom where platform UI overlaps)
