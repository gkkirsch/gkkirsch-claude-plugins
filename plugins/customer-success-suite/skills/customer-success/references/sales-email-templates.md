# Sales Email Templates — 50+ Ready-to-Use Templates by Pipeline Stage

Complete email templates organized by sales pipeline stage. Each template includes subject line variants, full body copy, personalization placeholders, and send timing. These are not outlines — they are actual emails you can personalize and send.

---

## 1. Cold Outreach Templates (12 Templates)

### Template 1.1: The Specific Observation
**Framework**: Curiosity Gap

**Subject Lines**:
1. `Quick question about {{COMPANY}}'s {{INITIATIVE}}`
2. `Noticed something about {{COMPANY}}`
3. `{{FIRST_NAME}} — re: {{SPECIFIC_OBSERVATION}}`

**Body**:
```
Hi {{FIRST_NAME}},

I was looking at {{COMPANY}}'s {{SPECIFIC_THING — job posting, product page, blog post, LinkedIn post}} and noticed {{OBSERVATION}}.

Most {{INDUSTRY}} companies I work with that {{SIMILAR_PATTERN}} end up {{NEGATIVE_CONSEQUENCE}} within {{TIMEFRAME}}.

We helped {{PEER_COMPANY}} avoid that — they {{SPECIFIC_RESULT}}.

Worth a 15-minute call to see if you're seeing the same thing?

{{YOUR_NAME}}
```

**Personalization Notes**: Research their company's website, recent job postings, tech stack, or public announcements. The observation must be genuine.

## Template Usage Guide

### Choosing the Right Template

| Situation | Recommended Template | Framework |
|-----------|---------------------|-----------|
| First cold touch, have research | 1.1 (Specific Observation) | Curiosity Gap |
| First cold touch, have case study | 1.2 (Peer Results) | Social Proof |
| Know their pain point | 1.3 (Pain Amplifier) | PAS |
| Recent trigger event | 1.4 (Trigger Event) | AIDA |
| C-suite outreach | 1.11 (Executive Outreach) | Value-First |
| Second touch | 2.1 (Value Add) | Value-First |
| Third touch | 2.2 (Social Proof) | Social Proof |
| Going dark | 2.3 (Breakup Tease) | PAS |
| Before demo | 3.1 (Demo Confirmation) | Direct |
| After demo | 3.6 (Post-Demo Recap) | Direct |
| Sending proposal | 4.1 (Proposal Delivery) | Direct |
| Deal stalled | 4.4 (Stalled Deal) | Direct |
| Price pushback | 5.1 (Price Objection) | Reframe |
| Already using competitor | 5.2 (Competitor) | Social Proof |
| Not the right time | 5.3 (Timing) | Options |
| Post-close | 6.1 (Welcome) | Direct |
| 90-day upsell | 6.2 (Check-In) | Value-First |
| Referral ask | 6.6 (Referral Request) | Direct |

### A/B Testing Priority

Test these elements in order (highest impact first):
1. **Subject line** — test 2 variants per campaign
2. **Opening line** — test hook vs. direct
3. **CTA format** — test question vs. statement
4. **Send time** — test morning vs. afternoon
5. **Email length** — test short (3 sentences) vs. standard (5 sentences)

### Personalization Depth Guide

| Level | What to Include | Time Required | Use When |
|-------|----------------|---------------|----------|
| **Basic** | Name, company, role | 30 seconds | High-volume outreach |
| **Standard** | + Industry, company size, pain point | 2 minutes | Targeted outreach |
| **Deep** | + Recent company news, tech stack, content they published | 5 minutes | Key accounts |
| **Custom** | + Specific observation, tailored insight, referenced their words | 10+ minutes | Enterprise/exec |
